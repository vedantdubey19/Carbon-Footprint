🔬 Calculation Methodology & Standards

Our emission estimates are calculated using internationally recognized carbon reporting standards:

  • Transport Factors: Sourced from the UK DEFRA (2025) Greenhouse Gas Reporting Conversion Factors (e.g., Petrol car average: 0.21 kg CO₂e/km).
  • Diet Factors: Sourced from Poore & Nemecek (Science, 2018) environmental impact studies (e.g., Beef averages 27.0 kg CO₂e/kg).
  • Grid Electricity Factors: Sourced from the Central Electricity Authority of India (CEA) CO₂ Baseline Database (2024) (avg: 0.82 kg CO₂e/kWh).
  • Shopping & Consumer Goods: Estimates based on life-cycle assessments (LCA) compiled by the World Resources Institute (WRI).

All estimates represent Carbon Dioxide Equivalent (CO₂e) emissions, accounting for methane (CH₄) and nitrous oxide (N₂O) impacts.
